# Image Placement Guide

Please add the following images to this folder:

## Required Images:

### 1. hero-bg.jpg
- **Purpose**: Main background image for the hero/landing section
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080 pixels (landscape)
- **Type**: Professional photo of your salon or nail work
- **Note**: This will be the first thing visitors see

### 2. gallery-1.jpg to gallery-6.jpg
- **Purpose**: Showcase your nail designs and beauty services
- **Recommended Size**: 800x800 pixels (square format)
- **Suggestions**:
  - gallery-1.jpg: Manicure work
  - gallery-2.jpg: Nail art designs
  - gallery-3.jpg: Acrylic/gel nails
  - gallery-4.jpg: Pedicure service
  - gallery-5.jpg: Beauty treatment
  - gallery-6.jpg: Another stunning design

## Image Tips:
✅ Use high-quality, well-lit photos
✅ Keep file sizes under 500KB for faster loading
✅ Use JPG format for photos
✅ Show variety in your work
✅ Ensure images are professional and clear

## After Adding Images:
Simply open the index.html file in your browser to see your website!

---

**Note**: If you want to use different image names, you can update the image paths in the index.html file.
